About Vision Institute of Science

Established in 2021 and located in Dharmabad, District Nanded, Maharashtra, Vision Institute of Science (VIS) is dedicated to providing high-quality science education. We offer specialized coaching for students of Class 9 & 10 (Science Foundation) and Class 11 & 12 (Biology & Chemistry), along with targeted preparation for competitive exams like NEET.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert faculty with 4+ years of teaching experience
  • Handwritten notes and personal mentorship
  • Result-oriented methodology to ensure academic excellence
  • Strong focus on conceptual clarity and problem-solving skills
  • Support for NEET preparation with extra Chemistry classes

Director & Founder

Mr. Mohammad Sultan, B.Sc. in Computer Science from Lal Bahadur Shastri Mahavidyalaya, Dharmabad. With over 4 years of teaching experience, he has successfully guided numerous students towards academic excellence and competitive exam success.

Our Vision

To be the leading institute in science education by empowering students with knowledge, discipline, and confidence to excel in academics and life.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality education through innovative teaching techniques, comprehensive notes, and a nurturing learning environment that prepares students for board exams and competitive challenges.

What We Provide

  • Detailed handwritten notes for all subjects
  • Chapter-wise tests and regular assessments
  • Doubt-solving sessions and revision classes
  • Motivational guidance and personal attention
  • Interactive learning with real-life examples
  • Complete NEET Chemistry preparation with concept building

Our Courses

We offer comprehensive science education programs tailored for different academic levels and competitive exam preparation.

Class 9 Science

Comprehensive foundation in Physics, Chemistry and Biology with NCERT syllabus coverage and practical applications.

Class 10 Science

Complete preparation for board exams with concept building, numerical practice and revision strategies.

Class 11 Biology

Detailed study of Botany and Zoology with diagrams, terminology and conceptual understanding.

Class 11 Chemistry

Physical, Organic and Inorganic Chemistry with numerical problem solving techniques.

Class 12 Biology

Advanced topics in Genetics, Evolution, Biotechnology and Human Physiology for board exams.

Class 12 Chemistry

Complete syllabus coverage with emphasis on organic reactions, coordination compounds and electrochemistry.

NEET Chemistry

Specialized coaching for NEET with focus on Physical, Organic and Inorganic Chemistry as per latest syllabus.
